About

Hey everyone! I’m Ahmed Sher otherwise known as Ace or crypt0ace. I’m a Cyber Security Nerd who loves to learn, break in and hack. I play a lot of Capture the Flag (CTFs) competitions with my team We Need Control. I also play TryHackMe and HackTheBox, Always learning cool new things. I’m more interested in Binary Exploitation and Reverse Engineering side of the CTFs. You can find my Walkthroughs of TryHackMe and HackTheBox machines, Write-Ups of CTFs I participate in and more great content right here on my blog.

If you want to contact me for anything, you can find my Twitter, LinkedIn and GitHub in the bottom left side of the footer. Alternatively, You can contact me on Discord at crypt0ace#3328\
